About Me

I'm Sathananthan, a dedicated and passionate UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer currently pursuing my Higher National Diploma in Information Technology. My mission is to design intuitive, user-centric interfaces that deliver seamless digital experiences.

I specialize in using tools like Figma, photoshop, and Canva for wireframing and prototyping, and I bring those designs to life using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React.

I've worked on real-world projects like a Smart vehicle service and a Face Recognition Attendance System, both of which helped me build strong problem-solving and UI decision-making skills.

I'm also deeply interested in responsive design, animation, and accessibility. Whether I'm designing a mobile-first layout or building a dark/light mode toggle, I love crafting products that not only look good but also work beautifully.
